Introducing Romeo
The painless attendance tracker

Why use Romeo?.

Fewer teacher distractions

Teachers can easily operate Romeo during class without diverting their attention from their students.

Improved attendance data quality

We know teachers are human and sometimes make mistakes when recording attendance data. Romeo catches these mistakes and makes it painless to correct them..

Fewer front office headaches

Romeo challenges outdated software by managing the attendance tracking process without significant involvement from front office staff and administrators.

How It Works

Teacher-Friendly Web Tool With Voice and Image Recognition

Open the attendance submission page for any of your classes with a single click from your computer or mobile device. Record attendance data by quickly tapping student name bars, speaking into a mobile device, or capturing an image of hand-written marks on a seating chart.

Reminders and Alerts

Reminders prompt you to submit your attendance on time, and alerts help you catch and resolve errors before the end of the school day.

Multiple Customization Options

Change the way student names are configured with a few easy clicks. Set your reminders and notifications to arrive at the most convenient times.
