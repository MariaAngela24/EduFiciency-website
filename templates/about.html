Meet Our Team

We’re on a mission improve education by making the technology teachers use more efficient.

Maria Mendiburo

CEO & Founder

Maria started her career in education as a mathematics teacher in Hawaii and went on to complete a MA and PhD in education at Stanford and Vanderbilt Universities. After completing a postdoc focused on edtech development and working with several edtech startups, she decided to spend another year teaching mathematics to reconnect with the day-to-day realities of classroom teaching. Her decision to found EduFiciency was inspired by that experience since she realized that trying to operate a computer system while simultaneously keeping an eye on 32 eighth graders is a lot like texting while driving.
