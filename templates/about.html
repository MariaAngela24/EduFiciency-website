Meet Our Team

Maria Mendiburo

CEO & Founder

Maria has a background in classroom teaching, academic research, and education technology development. She taught middle school mathematics in 2015-2016 after being out of the classroom for over a decade and realized that trying to operate a computer system while teaching is a lot like texting while driving. She founded EduFiciency to address that problem.

Jeff M

CIO & founder

Jeff has over 25 successful years of experience in the corporate world software technology filling every role from developer to executive leadership.  He is excited that working with EduFiciency has enabled him to get back to his technologist roots and immerse himself in a company he can help shape from the ground up.  He is a graduate of Purdue University and frequently makes the rest of the team listen to old school hip hop mixes during long hacking sessions.
